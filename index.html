About ASTRA

ASTRA is the official cybersecurity association of the B.Tech Cybersecurity program at KMCT Institute of Emerging Technologies and Management.

We introduce students to live classes, technical workshops, Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, and hands-on cybersecurity training. Our vision is to create a platform where students can collaborate, innovate, and grow into the next generation of ethical hackers and security professionals.
